What To Do If You Have Been Banned From a Sub

If you have been banned by a subreddit that OnlyPost posts to, then please follow the instructions below to remedy the situation and get OnlyPost back up and running. See the image below step 8 to see what your email should look like before sending it.

  1. Open up your email
  2. Start a new email draft
  3. Put our email address into the 'To:' field.
  4. Put "I Got Banned" in the subject field.
  5. Write in the email what sub you got banned from. Write something simple like "I was banned from r/thissubreddit"
  6. Double click the blue OnlyPost folder on your desktop to open it and see the files inside.
  7. Drag and drop the file titled "subs.py" into the bottom of the email you have open and are about to send us.
  8. Send the email and we will get back to you with a new subs.py file within 48 hours.





Once You Get an Email Back From Us

  1. Open up the blue OnlyPost folder on your desktop.
  2. Drag the subs.py file that is currently in your OnlyPost folder and drop it into the trash.
  3. Drag the new subs.py file at the bottom of the email we sent you onto your desktop.
  4. Drag the new sub.py file that is now on your desktop and drop it in the blue OnlyPost folder.
  5. Thats it! You're all done and you can resume using OnlyPost to post to Reddit.
