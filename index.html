What To Do If You Have Been Banned From a Sub

If you have been banned by a subreddit that OnlyPost posts to, then please follow the instructions below to remedy the situation and get OnlyPost back up and running.

  1. Open up your email
  2. Put our email address into the To: field.
  3. Put "I got banned" in the subject field.
  4. Double click the blue OnlyPost folder on your desktop to open it and see the files inside
  5. Drag and drop the file titled "subs.py" into the email draft
  6. Send the email and we will get back to you with a new subs.py file within 48 hours.
